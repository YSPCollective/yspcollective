Common Questions

Everything you need to know

What condition are your beauty products in?+
All beauty, skincare and fragrance products are brand new, sealed, and within date. We source exclusively from authorised EU distributors — no grey market stock. Every fragrance comes with full batch traceability.
Where do you ship to?+
We ship across Europe from our base in Portugal. Most orders dispatch within 24 hours. Delivery is typically 2–5 business days depending on destination. No hidden import costs within the EU.
Are your K-beauty products authentic?+
100%. We source from authorised distributors who import directly and legally into the EU. Every product is genuine, correctly labelled for the EU market, and within its stated shelf life. We stake our reputation on authenticity.
Why Arabian fragrances?+
Arabian fragrance houses like Lattafa and Armaf create rich, long-lasting compositions that rival designer fragrances at a fraction of the price. These aren't imitations — they're original, expertly crafted scents with 8-12+ hour longevity that have built a massive global following. We source through official EU distributors so every bottle is guaranteed authentic.
Do you offer returns?+
Yes. If there's any issue with your order, get in touch within 14 days and we'll arrange a return or replacement. We stand behind everything we sell.
Can I buy in bulk or wholesale?+
Absolutely — we welcome trade and bulk enquiries across all product lines. Contact us with your requirements and we'll come back with availability and pricing.
Where can I find you on Amazon?+
Our products are available across Amazon EU marketplaces under the seller name "YSP Collective". You can also find us on eBay or purchase directly through this site.
