There's a reason Club de Nuit Intense Man has become one of the most talked-about fragrances on the internet — and it has nothing to do with its price.


Launched in 2015, this woody-spicy fragrance opens with a bright, energetic burst of lemon, pineapple, bergamot, black currant, and apple Fragrantica — a citrus-fruit opening that's confident and immediately attention-grabbing.


The heart softens into birch, jasmine, and rose, adding a masculine depth that keeps things from ever feeling sharp or one-dimensional. Then the base takes over: a smooth, full-bodied foundation of ambergris, musk, patchouli, and vanilla Amazon that lingers long after everything else has settled.


Performance is where this fragrance has built its reputation. Projection is strong in the first few hours, longevity regularly hits the 8–12 hour mark on skin, and the dry-down — smoky, warm, and quietly sophisticated — is genuinely excellent. It's the kind of fragrance people notice and ask about.


CDNIM (as it's known in fragrance circles) is widely regarded as one of the finest value propositions in men's fragrance. Designer houses charge multiples of this price for comparable performance and complexity. At this price point, it's almost unreasonably good.
Suitable for evening wear, cooler weather, and any occasion where you want to leave an impression.
