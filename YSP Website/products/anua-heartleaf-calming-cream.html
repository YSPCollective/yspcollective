YSP's Thoughts

Why we carry this

Finding a moisturiser that actually calms irritated skin — without feeling like you've applied a thick paste — is harder than it should be. This ANUA cream gets it right. It's rich enough to properly hydrate but absorbs quickly with no greasy finish.

The 70% Heartleaf concentration is genuinely high — this isn't a token amount for the label. Heartleaf (Houttuynia Cordata) is a staple in Korean skincare for a reason: it's naturally anti-inflammatory and helps reduce redness and irritation without any harsh actives.

We particularly recommend this for anyone with sensitive skin who's been let down by "calming" moisturisers that don't actually calm anything. It pairs brilliantly with the ANUA Cleansing Oil as a two-step evening routine. Simple, effective, no nonsense — that's exactly what we look for.

— The YSP Collective Team

Product Details

ANUA Heartleaf 70 Intense Calming Cream is a soothing moisturiser formulated with 70% Heartleaf (Houttuynia Cordata) extract. It's designed to deliver intensive hydration while calming redness, irritation and sensitivity.

The cream has a balanced, gel-cream texture that absorbs smoothly without leaving a heavy or tacky finish. It works to strengthen the skin barrier over time while providing immediate comfort to stressed skin.

Suitable for all skin types, particularly beneficial for sensitive, combination and redness-prone skin. Can be used morning and evening as the final step in your routine before SPF.
