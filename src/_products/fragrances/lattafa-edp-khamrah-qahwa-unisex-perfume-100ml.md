description_full: >
  If Khamrah is a spiced Arabian evening, Qahwa is that same evening with a
  coffee in hand — and it might be even better.


  Launched in 2023 as a sibling to the original Khamrah, Qahwa opens with cinnamon, cardamom, and ginger — an intensely spiced, almost gingerbread-like opening that's warm and immediate. The heart moves into praline, candied fruits, and white flowers, building a creamy, sweetly complex core. Then the base arrives and everything clicks: coffee, vanilla, tonka bean, benzoin, and musk settle into a rich, deeply gourmand finish that's cosy without ever feeling heavy-handed.


  The coffee note is the masterstroke. Rather than dominating, it emerges gradually as the fragrance develops on skin — the longer it sits, the more pronounced it becomes, grounding the sweetness of the praline and spices in something darker and more sophisticated. The overall effect is somewhere between a spiced coffee shop and an Arabian confectionery — warming, distinctive, and genuinely hard to put down.


  Performance is exceptional — longevity of 14–15 hours on skin and days on clothing is regularly reported, with strong projection in the first few hours. A Fragrantica Readers' Choice Award winner for 2024, and one of the most talked-about gourmand fragrances at any price point.


  Best worn in autumn and winter. One or two sprays maximum.
