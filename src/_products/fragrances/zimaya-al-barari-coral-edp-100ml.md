description_full: |
  <p>Al Barari Coral opens bright and immediately inviting: bergamot and orange set the tone, with mango and pear adding a ripe, juicy sweetness that feels natural rather than candy-like. A hint of ginger runs through the opening — just enough to give it edge and stop it reading as a simple fruity scent. The heart develops gently; orange blossom brings a delicate floral note without sweetness, while red berries and cedar add depth and a subtle woody structure that anchors the composition. It's a sophisticated treatment of what could easily have become a generic summer fragrance.</p>
  <p>The dry-down on cedar, amber and musk is soft and skin-close — warm without being heavy, musky without being overbearing. It's the kind of dry-down that makes you want to spray it again. Performance is moderate at around 6 hours on skin, which is typical for a light fruity EDP — a little more on clothing. Projection is polite and close-range, making it an ideal office or daytime option.</p>
  <p>Part of Zimaya's nature-inspired Al Barari collection — 'Al Barari' translates as 'wilderness' in Arabic, and the line is designed to capture the organic warmth of the Emirates. Zimaya operates under the established Afnan Perfumes group, which guarantees consistent quality.</p>
