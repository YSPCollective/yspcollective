description_full: |
  <p>Club de Nuit Intense Man needs little introduction — it's been one of the most discussed masculine fragrances of the last decade. The Parfum concentration in 150ml is the best version Armaf have produced of this DNA.</p>
  <p>The opening lands with the characteristic burst of apple, pineapple and bergamot sharpened with blackcurrant. Lively and citrus-forward, with a hint of lemon. The opening is bolder than you might expect from a Parfum, but it clears quickly into the heart, which is where this concentration distinguishes itself. Birch — the smoky, distinctive note at the soul of this fragrance — arrives with more depth and less sharpness than in the EDT, sitting alongside rose and jasmine in a way that feels genuinely refined rather than simply powerful. The dry-down on patchouli, musk and vanilla is warm, earthy and addictive: long-wearing, intimate and confident.</p>
  <p>Multiple reviewers who have owned every concentration rate the Parfum as the most complete version, smoother than the EDT, more characterful than the EDP. At 150ml, it's also exceptional value per ml. Longevity is reliably in the 10–14 hour range.</p>
ysp_thoughts: |
  <p>Creed Aventus is a genuine icon — smoky birch, blackcurrant, pineapple and that warm base that follows you around all day. It also costs upwards of €300 for 100ml and has had well-documented consistency issues over the years.</p>
  <p>Club de Nuit Intense Man has been the fragrance community's answer to that for nearly a decade. The Parfum concentration at 150ml is the version to get: reviewers who own Aventus and every CDNIM concentration consistently point to this one as the most satisfying daily wear. The patchouli in the base adds an earthiness and smoothness the other concentrations lack.</p>
  <p>If you've been wearing the 105ml EDP, the step up to the Parfum is immediately noticeable. If you're new to this fragrance, start here.</p>
