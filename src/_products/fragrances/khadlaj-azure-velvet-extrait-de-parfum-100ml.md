description_full: >
  <p>Azure Velvet is one of Khadlaj's most acclaimed releases, and the reason is
  straightforward: it captures the spirit of Parfums de Marly Layton — one of
  the benchmark aromatic woody fragrances of the last decade — at a fraction of
  the cost, and in extrait concentration.</p>

  <p>The opening is immediately appealing: bergamot, lavender and a clean hit of mint create a fresh, invigorating introduction that feels polished rather than sporty. The mint here is light and natural, giving Azure Velvet a slightly cooler, airier quality than the original Layton, which some wearers actually prefer.</p>

  <p>The heart develops beautifully — floral and fruity notes emerge with a soft, almost velvety quality, complemented by the fresh character that keeps the fragrance from feeling heavy mid-wear. This is the stage where Azure Velvet most closely echoes Layton's warmth and gentle sweetness, without tipping into gourmand territory.</p>

  <p>The extrait base is where the real quality shows. Woody notes, soft powder and a hint of incense settle into a rich, lasting dry-down that carries for 7–10 hours and projects confidently without being loud. As an extrait de parfum, the concentration gives it a depth and complexity that distinguishes it from cheaper alternatives in this space.</p>

  <p>Fragrantica reviewers have called it the best Layton dupe on the market. Wearers of Layton have said they prefer it. Either way, it's exceptional value.</p>
