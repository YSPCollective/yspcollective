  <p>Island Dreams opens with a burst of bergamot and ginger — bright, slightly tart, immediately energising. The citrus character doesn't fade here; it's reinforced at the heart by a vivid grapefruit accord that keeps things feeling fresh and alive rather than flat. As it dries down, ambroxan and musk create a smooth, addictive trail that sits close to the skin without disappearing. Multiple wearers report 8–10 hours easily, with the dry-down lingering well into the evening. It's a minimalist composition — only a handful of notes — but every one earns its place.
