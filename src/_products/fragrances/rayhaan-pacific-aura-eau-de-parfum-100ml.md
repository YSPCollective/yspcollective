  <p>Pacific Aura is Rayhaan's entry into one of the most celebrated scent
  territories of recent years — the coastal citrus-mint space popularised by
  Louis Vuitton Pacific Chill. The fragrance community has widely noted the

  <p>The opening is vivid and immediately refreshing: citron, mandarin, mint and bergamot arrive in a bright, clean burst. Black currant adds a juicy, slightly tart sweetness and coriander introduces a subtle spice that stops the opening from being one-dimensional. It smells like the best version of a summer morning.</p>

  <p>The heart is where the real sophistication lives. Aromatic basil, rose and carrot seed create a green, herbal complexity that's unusual in this category — it grounds the freshness and gives Pacific Aura an identity beyond just "clean and citrusy."</p>

  <p>The base delivers on longevity where many fresh fragrances disappoint. Amber adds warmth, fig brings a creamy softness, and ambroxan extends the whole composition on skin for 7–10 hours — considerably longer than most aquatic-style EDPs. Projection is also notably stronger than the LV original, making Pacific Aura a better choice for outdoor wear or situations where presence matters.</p>
