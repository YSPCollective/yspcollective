What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, function more efficiently, and to provide information to the site owner. Cookies are not harmful and cannot run programmes or deliver viruses.

How We Use Cookies

We use a small number of cookies, grouped into two categories: essential cookies that the site cannot function without, and optional analytics cookies that we only set with your consent.

Manage your preferences
You can change your cookie consent at any time using our cookie preference centre.

Essential Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. They do not track you or store any personally identifiable information.

Requires Consent

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site — which pages are popular, how people arrive, and where they leave. This helps us improve the site. These cookies are only set if you accept analytics cookies via our consent banner.

Google Analytics data is anonymised (IP anonymisation is enabled) and is not used to identify you personally. We do not sell this data to any third party.

Third-Party Cookies

Third Party

Our website includes a Trustpilot review widget in the footer. Trustpilot may set their own cookies when this widget loads. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to Trustpilot's Privacy Policy for details.

Our email marketing is handled by Brevo (Sendinblue). Brevo processes email data server-side only and does not set any cookies on your browser through our website.

What We Don't Do

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or profiling. We do not share cookie data with advertisers. We do not use any social media tracking pixels.

How to Control Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences for this site at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in our website footer, or using the button above.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the site. Useful links:

Google Chrome · Mozilla Firefox · Safari · Microsoft Edge

To opt out of Google Analytics across all websites, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page with the date of last update. If we make significant changes, we will reset consent so you are asked again.
